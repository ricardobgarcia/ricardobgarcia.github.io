About me

Ricardo Basso Garcia

I have Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Linguistics (State University of Campinas, UNICAMP, Brazil) and Ph.D. in Sciences/Psychobiology (University of Sao Paulo, USP, Brazil). As a Ph.D exchange student and Postdoctoral visiting scholar, I have worked for two years in the Memory and Learning Lab at the University of Padua, Italy.

During my undergraduate studies, I became interested in Artificial Intelligence and cognition, so during my master I conducted research on Natural Language Processing using Recurrent Neural Networks. This gave me experience with neural networks, the error backpropagation algorithm, as well as with multivariate statistical methods, such as Cluster Analysis and Principal Component Analysis.

During my Ph.D., I conducted experimental research in the field of human cognition, investigating memory, verbal and visuospatial skills in adults and children with learning disabilities. This gave me experience with hypothesis testing and classification methods, as well as with programming, since I developed memory tests and games. The quality of my work and writing abilities contributed to the publication of several articles in high-quality scientific journals.

Since my Ph.D., I have been an academic researcher, and a consultant on programming, data analysis, and scientific writing for the academic sector. I am looking for an opportunity to work professionally as a Data Scientist. I have been working on personal projects on Data Science in order to gain experience in solving business problems and improve the most current tools in the area of data analysis and Machine Learning.
